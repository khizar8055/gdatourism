## Presentation Tips

### Preparation
- **Rehearse** the presentation multiple times
- **Test** the live demo thoroughly
- **Prepare** backup slides for technical issues
- **Anticipate** questions and prepare answers
- **Bring** business cards and handouts

### Delivery
- **Start strong** with a compelling opening
- **Speak clearly** and at a moderate pace
- **Make eye contact** with the audience
- **Use gestures** to emphasize points
- **Show enthusiasm** for the project

### Visuals
- **Keep slides** simple and uncluttered
- **Use high-quality** images and graphics
- **Maintain consistency** in design
- **Limit text** on slides
- **Use animations** sparingly

### Q&A
- **Listen carefully** to questions
- **Answer concisely** and directly
- **Admit** if you don't know an answer
- **Follow up** after the presentation
- **Stay calm** and confident
